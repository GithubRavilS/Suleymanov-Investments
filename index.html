Risk & Stability (Drawdown Dynamics)

Clear expectations for institutional allocators.

  • Strategy generates consistent fee income regardless of market direction.
  • Primary risk is temporary capital drawdown, not yield degradation.
  • In volatile or trending markets, positions may experience -10% to -30% unrealized drawdown due to price movement and impermanent loss.
  • Fee generation continues during drawdowns and historically compensates these effects over time.
  • Break-even period depends on volatility and ranges, typically 1–3 months.
